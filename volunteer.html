Join our team of Rescue Ranch volunteers!

At Rescue Ranch, we save dogs. We give them first-rate care, love, and comfort. We also do everything in our power to find each of them a loving home.


All of our dogs need clean kennels, food and water, individualized attention, play time, training, walks and more. Some also require medical attention, transport, special training and/or fostering.


In addition to working directly with the dogs, volunteers can support our mission by:

  1. engaging in activities related to with office administration
  2. data entry
  3. promotion
  4. fundraising
  5. adoption events
  6. fostering
  7. volunteer coordination
  8. communications
  9. and so much more!

So, as you can see, it takes a lot of people to operate Rescue Ranch. We couldn't possibly do it all without the support of our amazing volunteers. We're so grateful for your interest in joining our team. Together we can save lives!
